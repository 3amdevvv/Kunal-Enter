About us

Established in 2002, M/S KUNAL ENTERPRISES specializes in supply and installation electronic equipment for monitoring and control of electrical power parameters. The proprietor, with an electronic engineering from Pune in 1988, has diverse experience at M/S FINOLEX CABLES LTD and TATA INSTITUTE OF FUNDAMENTAL RESEARCH. Joined M/S MINILEC INDIA PVT LTD in 1993, advancing from sales engineer to area manager, overseeing Mumbai, Gujarat, and Asia Pacific Countries.
